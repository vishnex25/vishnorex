Comprehensive Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy statement is issued by Vishnorex Technologies Pvt Ltd and its global subsidiaries (collectively, "Vishnorex", "we", "us" or "our").

Part I – Information Vishnorex Collects and Controls

1. Information You Actively Provide

We only collect the information that we actually need to conduct business with you.

  • Account Registration: When you create an account to access Vishnorex services, we collect your full name, professional email address, contact number, company name, and geographic location. This allows us to verify your identity and provide localized support.
  • Payment & Billing: When you purchase a subscription or product, we require billing details. While we collect your name and address for tax/legal compliance, Vishnorex does not store raw credit card numbers on our servers. All transactions are handled via PCI-DSS compliant gateways. With your explicit consent, we may store an encrypted "token" to facilitate easier future renewals.
  • Direct Interactions: We record data from your participation in surveys, contests, or newsletter subscriptions. If you contact our customer support via www.vishnorex.com, we may log the conversation to ensure your issue is resolved and to train our AI support models for better future accuracy.

2. Information Collected Automatically

To ensure the security and performance of our infrastructure, we collect technical data:

  • Device & Browser Metadata: We log your IP address, browser type (e.g., Chrome, Safari), operating system, and hardware model. This helps us optimize our software for the devices our customers actually use.
  • Usage Analytics: We track how you interact with www.vishnorex.com, including page clickstreams, time spent on specific features, and the referring URL (how you found us).
  • Cookie Policy: We use first-party cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. Vishnorex maintains a strict "Anti-Intrusion" policy: we do not use third-party tracking cookies that follow you across the web for advertising purposes.

3. Information from Third Parties

  • Social Authentication: If you use "Sign in with Google" or LinkedIn, those platforms provide us with a secure token and your basic profile info (email and name) to create your Vishnorex account.
  • Marketing Partners: We may receive contact information from authorized resellers or event organizers if you have expressed interest in Vishnorex products at a trade show or webinar.

Part II – Information Vishnorex Processes on Your Behalf (Service Data)

This section applies when you use Vishnorex tools to manage your own business data (e.g., your customer lists, employee payroll, or files).

1. Ownership and Stewardship

  • Data Ownership: You remain the sole owner of any data you upload or generate within our apps. Vishnorex acts strictly as a Data Processor.
  • Privacy by Design: We do not look at your data unless you explicitly invite us to (e.g., for a support ticket). Our employees' access is logged, audited, and restricted via "Least Privilege" protocols.
  • Mobile Permissions: If you use the Vishnorex mobile app, we may request access to your camera or contacts. This data is processed locally to perform the action you requested (like scanning a receipt) and is only synced to our servers if you choose to save it there.

2. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Active Period: We store your service data as long as your account is active.
  • Post-Termination: If you cancel your service, your data remains in our "Active Database" for 6 months to allow for easy reactivation. After 6 months, it is purged.
  • Backups: Once purged from the active database, data may persist in encrypted backups for an additional 3 months before being permanently overwritten.

Part III – General Global Standards

1. Global Data Rights (GDPR & Beyond)

Regardless of your country of residence, Vishnorex grants you the following rights:

  • Right to Portability: You can export your data in machine-readable formats (JSON, CSV) at any time.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request the "Right to be Forgotten," and we will delete your personal data from our systems, provided it is not required for legal or tax compliance.
  • Right to Object: You can opt-out of any processing based on "Legitimate Interests" by contacting our Data Protection Officer at www.vishnorex.com.

2. AI and Machine Learning Transparency

Vishnorex uses AI to provide features like "Smart Predictions" and "Automated Data Entry."

  • We do not train our global AI models using your private, identifiable service data.
  • We use anonymized, aggregated datasets to improve our algorithms. Any AI model built specifically for your organization stays within your account boundary.

3. Security and Compliance

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2/1.3 and at rest using AES-256.
  • Legal Disclosures: We will only disclose your data to government authorities if required by a valid, legally binding warrant or subpoena. We will notify you of such requests unless legally prohibited from doing so.

4. Policy Updates

Vishnorex Technologies Pvt Ltd reserves the right to modify this policy.

  • Major Changes: We will provide 30 days' notice via email before significant changes take effect.
  • Minor Changes: Non-material changes (like fixing typos) will be updated directly on www.vishnorex.com/privacy.
